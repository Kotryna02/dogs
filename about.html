About Us

Furry Friends: FURRY FRIENDS TRAINING AND CARE Clinic offers a range of services to care for your pet's grooming, spa, and wellness needs. Our primary goal is to provide complete care for your pet from the moment they enter our facility. Each pet that visits us receives a complimentary exam, and we provide a personalized report with recommendations for at-home care. At Furr, we focus on preventive care and strive to keep your pet healthy. Additionally, we understand the importance of style and can assist you in giving your pet a unique look. Our experienced groomers are certified and knowledgeable in all aspects of pet grooming, including nail painting and mohawk styling, to ensure your furry friend looks their best.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to ensuring the safety, style, comfort, and well-being of your pets. At Furry Friends, we provide exceptional customer service and individualized care to each pet that we welcome.
